Family Law Act Protection Orders

Use this service to apply for a Protection Order under the Family Law Act from the Provincial Court of British Columbia. This service cannot be used to apply for a Protection Order from the Supreme Court of British Columbia.

Call 9-1-1 if you are in immediate danger.

Please use a safer computer (for example, at a Public Library).

If you are not in immediate danger but are still afraid for your safety VictimLinkBC can help you develop a personal safety plan.

Call: 1-800-563-0808 | TTY: 604-875-0885 | Text: 604-863-6381


Time: The average time to complete this service is 30-45 minutes. This service will save your information and you can come back to it any time.

If your situation is urgent and you need a Protection Order right away, a judge may hear your application the same day you file it at a courthouse. If your situation is not urgent, the application will take more time.

Cost: This service is free. The Government of British Columbia does not charge fees for Family Law cases in the Provincial Court of British Columbia.

Print: You will need to print your application and file it at a Provincial Courthouse. At this time, you cannot use this service to file electronically.
